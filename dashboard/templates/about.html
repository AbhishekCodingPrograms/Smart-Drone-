The Technology Behind It

Our platform is powered by state-of-the-art convolutional neural networks (CNN) trained on thousands of crop images. The AI model can distinguish between healthy and diseased crops with 87% accuracy, enabling early intervention and preventing crop loss.

Combined with NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index) analysis from satellite imagery, our system provides comprehensive insights into crop health, moisture levels, and growth patterns. The autonomous drone system ensures efficient field coverage with minimal human intervention.
